About Us

In 1924, Sigma Chapter became the seventh chapter of Theta Tau, the first and foremost engineering fraternity in the country. Throughout the years, over 1000 young men have been initiated into this truly unique brotherhood. Every brother learns and lives out the core principles of brotherhood, professional development, and service.

Professional

From military legends to Fortune 500 CEOs, Theta Tau boasts a network of successful alumni in all kinds of engineering fields. Sigma Chapter fosters academic success through study groups, a wealth of resources such as textbooks, and personal help from brothers who have experience with intreviews, resumes, hard classes, etc.

Brotherhood

Foster a lifelong sense of brotherhood among other engineers.
